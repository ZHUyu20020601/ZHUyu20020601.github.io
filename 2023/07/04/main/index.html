Experience

  • Diffusion Attack Research

    I joined a group of three in usslab carrying out a research to exam the posibility of backdoor attack using diffusion inpainting. To promote the attack success rate, we adopt the incremental learning such as EWC and MAS. Though we haven’t overcome all the barriers, we do find the strategy effective. In this experience I learnt knowledges of SOTA AI attack/defense and AI techniques.

  • Image-Classification

    A final course project of AI. We adopted the MindSpore Lite framekwork which is created by HUAWEI and fine-tuned a mobilenet model via cloud, after which we load the model to an mobile phone application. The dataset is Animal10 which we download from Kaggle and prepressed.

  • News Material Classification
    The final project of course AI & ML, whose task is to classify a dataset of Chinese news. By adopting the method word2vec instead of traditional embedding method, and using text-cnn as the backbone, I reached a good mark in classification task. Our team tried using pretrained bert model for higher preformance.

  • Auto Coffee Machine
    The final project of course advenced embedding system. We use the STM32 as the central controller to manipulate actuators like the relays and heaters, via the operation system FreeRTOS. An ESP32 is also used to communicate with the cloud and STM32 in order to control the machine in distance.

  • Experiment on convolution and Correlation
    The final project of course signal analysis and process, in which I conducted experiments on the relationship between convolution and correlation. By comparing the foluma and the outcome I found that convolution is the reverse pattern of correlation, yet they have quite similar functions.
